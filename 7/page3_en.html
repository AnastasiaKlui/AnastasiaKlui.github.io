Musical Terms Starting with the Letter B

Home Musical Terms Famous Musical Works Categories of Musical Instruments Pool Write to us

Term Meaning
Ballad a lyrical or epic musical work, often with lyrics that tell a story
Baritone a male voice of medium range, between tenor and bass
Bass the lowest male voice or instrumental register
Flat a symbol that lowers a note by a half step
Recorder a wooden wind instrument with a simple sound-producing mechanism
Bolero a Spanish dance of medium tempo, also a musical genre
Bravo an exclamation to express admiration and support for a performer after a successful performance

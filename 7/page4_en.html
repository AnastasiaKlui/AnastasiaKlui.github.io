Musical Terms Starting with the Letter V

    Home Musical Terms Famous Musical Works Categories of Musical Instruments Survey Contact Us

Waltz – a dance in triple meter with smooth movements, common in classical and ballroom music.

Vibrato – a technique of oscillating pitch to add expressiveness to the sound.

Vocal – the art of singing or the vocal part in a musical composition.

Volta – a musical symbol indicating the repetition of a section of the composition with variations or a change of ending.

Cello – a stringed instrument with a low pitch.

Virtuoso – a musician who possesses extraordinary skill in playing an instrument or singing.

Introduction – the introductory section of a musical work, preparing the main theme or melody.

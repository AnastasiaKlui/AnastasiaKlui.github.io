Musical Terms Starting with G

    Home Musical Terms Famous Musical Works Musical Instrument Categories Mathematical Operations Survey Contact Us

Scale – a sequence of musical notes (sounds) that follow each other in pitch.

Hymn – a solemn song or musical composition that glorifies a particular idea, country, or person.

Glissando – a smooth transition from one note to another by sliding.

Gracioso – a musical indication to perform with grace and lightness.

Oboe – a woodwind instrument with a double reed.

Gravicord – a stringed keyboard instrument, the predecessor of the modern piano.

Harmonica – a musical instrument that produces sound by vibrating air inside its body when air is blown or drawn through holes.

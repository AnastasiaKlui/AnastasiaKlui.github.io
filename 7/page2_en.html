Musical Terms Starting with A

    Home Musical Terms Famous Musical Works Categories of Musical Instruments Poll Contact Us

A Cappella – vocal performance without instrumental accompaniment.

Alto – medium female or children's voice; also refers to the corresponding instrumental register.

Allegretto – moderately fast tempo, slightly slower than allegro.

Allegro – fast and lively tempo.

Andante – calm, moderate tempo, literally means "walking."

Ensemble – a group of musicians performing together, or a coordinated performance.

Aria – a solo vocal composition, usually with instrumental or orchestral accompaniment.

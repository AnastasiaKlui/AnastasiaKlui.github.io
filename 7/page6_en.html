Term Meaning
Discant a high voice, usually a child's or a woman's, performing the higher parts in a choir
Diminuendo a male voice of medium range between tenor and bass
Conductor the leader of an orchestra or choir who coordinates the performance of music
Dissonance a combination of sounds that creates tension or a sense of instability
Diversion a musical piece or part of a piece with a light, entertaining character
12-Tone Technique a musical technique that uses all 12 notes of the chromatic scale equally
Duet a musical piece or performance for two performers

Term Meaning
Exposition the first part of sonata form, where the main musical themes are presented
Impromptu a musical composition performed or created without prior preparation
Etude a short musical composition intended to improve the performer’s technique
Enharmonicism a phenomenon where two different notes sound the same but are written differently (e.g., C# and Db)
Elegy a musical composition of a lyrical, melancholic character
Ensemble a small group of performers who play or sing together
Expression emotional expressiveness in music, a way to convey feelings through performance
